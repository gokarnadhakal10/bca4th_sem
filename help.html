Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote?

To register for voting, follow these steps:

1. Click on "Register" in the main navigation
2. Fill out the registration form with your student details
3. Upload required documents for verification
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
5. Wait for admin approval (usually within 24 hours)
6. Once approved, you can log in and vote

How do I cast my vote?

Casting your vote is simple:

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to the "Vote" section
3. Review the list of candidates
4. Select your preferred candidate for each position
5. Confirm your selection
6. Submit your vote
7. You will receive a confirmation message

Note: You can only vote once per election period.

Is my vote anonymous and secure?

Yes, absolutely! Our system ensures:

• Complete Anonymity: Your vote is not linked to your personal identity
• End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission
• Secure Servers: Votes are stored on highly secure servers
• Audit Trail: System maintains integrity without compromising privacy
• No Vote Modification: Once submitted, votes cannot be altered

We use industry-standard security protocols to protect your privacy.

What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Click the reset link and create a new password
6. Log in with your new credentials

If you don't receive the reset email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, for security and integrity reasons, votes cannot be changed once submitted. This prevents:

• Vote manipulation
• Multiple voting attempts
• Election fraud

Please review your selections carefully before submitting. If you encounter issues before submission, you can restart the voting process.

When will election results be announced?

Election results will be announced according to this schedule:

• Voting Period: Usually 24-48 hours
• Result Processing: Immediately after voting closes
• Result Announcement: Within 2 hours of voting completion
• Official Declaration: Posted on dashboard and notice board

You will receive a notification when results are available. All times are based on your institution's schedule.
