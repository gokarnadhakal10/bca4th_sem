Online Voting System - Help

Welcome to the Online Voting System! This page will guide you on how to register, login, vote, and resolve common issues.

1. Registration

  • Go to the Registration Page.
  • Fill in all required fields: Name, Email, Mobile Number, Password, Confirm Password.
  • Passwords must be at least 8 characters long.
  • Click Submit to complete registration.
  • Note: Each email must be unique.

2. Login

  • Go to the Login Page.
  • Enter your registered email and password.
  • Select your role: voter or admin.
  • If login fails, check your email, password, and role.
  • Forgot your password? Contact support.

3. Voting

  • Select the position you want to vote for (President, Vice President, Class CR).
  • Choose your preferred candidate and click Submit.
  • Each voter can vote only once per position.
  • Your vote is confidential and secure.

4. Admin Functions

  • Admins can add, edit, or remove candidates.
  • Admins can view voting results.
  • Admins can manage registered users.
  • Keep admin credentials secure and do not share with others.

5. Security and Privacy

  • Do not share your password with anyone.
  • All votes are confidential and encrypted.
  • Use secure devices and networks to vote.

6. Troubleshooting

  • Login issues: Ensure your email, password, and role are correct.
  • Page not loading: Clear browser cache or try a different browser.
  • Forgot password: Contact support for help.
